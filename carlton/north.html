Rosalie Moller

SS Rosalie Moller (Capt. Byrne), with a cargo of British coals, was sunk in exactly the same way as the Thistlegorm, but ony 2 days later. She had done a similar voyage, rounding South Africa, to get to the Red Sea. In fact, the planes that spotted the Thistlegorm, reported more valuable targets when returning from their raid, one of them being the Rosalie Moller. There were 2 casualties.
